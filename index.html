Aubrey "Drake" Graham is a Canadian rapper, singer, songwriter, and actor who has become one of the most dominant forces in popular music. Since his breakout mixtape "So Far Gone" in 2009, he has shattered countless records, earning multiple Grammy Awards and consistently topping the Billboard charts.

Known for his emotional vulnerability, versatile flows, and knack for creating global hits, Drake has redefined the sound of modern hip-hop and pop. His influence extends beyond music into fashion and business with his OVO (October's Very Own) brand.
